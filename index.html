    Healthcare Data Mart with SCD0

    My role

    20% as Corporate BI Architect
    80% as BI Technical responsible, Scrum Master and Development Team

    Previous existing product characteristics

    No analytical product existed.
    All analytical reporting came from different, independent operational products (silos). Due to that:
    • They had big performance and scalability problems
    • They impacted heavily in ‘normal’ operational products performance
    • They had critical data integration problems

    New product requirements (stakeholders view)

    Product Owner wanted a complete Healthcare analytical solution for all clinical activity data.
    Data should have a maximum delay of 24 hours.
    Solution should be able to integrate tents of independent operational products.

    New product characteristics

    A SCD0 Healthcare Data Mart was created with clinical activity data.
    Kimball methodology was used for modelling the business processes (Dimensional modelling).
    All main business requirements were implemented.

    In-house Registry Master Data

    My role

    20% as Corporate BI Architect
    80% as BI Technical responsible, Scrum Master and Development Team

    Previous existing product characteristics

    No MDM product existed neither a similar integrated alike product.
    Tens of operational products existed as silos.
    Integrated data analysis was a really big challenge.

    New product requirements (stakeholders view)

    Product Owner wanted a MDM system in order to integrate different operational Master Data.
    The system should infer (as much as possible) the master data mapping between operational products master data.
    For version 1 no specific human resource existed in order to validate or set master data mapping between operational systems.

    New product characteristics

    An in-house MDM system registry type was created.
    For version 1 heuristic rules were applied in order to match as many codes and descriptions as possible between tens of master tables from operational products.
    The product created was defined by stakeholders as ‘a huge step forward’ regarding data integration.
    This solution was the base for the first Data Mart of the company and the creation of the first integrated analytical solution.

    In-house Registry and Centralized Master Data

    My role

    Data Architect and Analyst

    Previous existing product characteristics

    A simple in house MDM alike system existed but due to technical and business limitations wasn’t used.
    Besides the known limitations an enormous list of new requirements were waiting to be developed.

    Data Masking

    My role

    20% as Corporate BI Architect
    80% as BI Technical responsible, Scrum Master and Development Team

    Previous existing product characteristics

    No Data Masking solution existed in any analytical solution (or similar).
    Data used is considered highly sensitive (EU standards).

    New product requirements (stakeholders view)

    As part of the Data Governance policy, Product Owner wanted that specific attributes should be masked. Specifically:
    • For the entire development environment (wherever the case)
    • For preproduction / validation / testing environment
    • To all production roles except those specified. Independently if data is seen in corporate reporting or self-service analysis
    An unmasking service should exist for unmasking data. The use of this service can only be done with previous consent and all details are logged for security reasons.

    New product characteristics

    A masking process was created using one-way hash function.
    The masking process was managed and executed by the infrastructure group.
    All main business requirements were implemented.

    Corporate Self-Service

    My role

    BI Architect, Analyst and Developer

    Previous existing product characteristics

    Previous reporting tool wasn’t updated for several years and had several known limitations.
    Only centralized reports from IT department could be publish to the organization.
    Only reports and analysis created in excel where share using email or file repositories.

    New product requirements (stakeholders view)

    Product Owner wanted a self-service solution that could connect to existing and new Multidimensional, Tabular and Dimensional models.
    The new analytical corporate self-service solution should be created as part of the centralized corporate reporting solution (same tool).
    Self-Service publication inside each departments and areas was needed.
    For 90% of the use cases the solution should be completely autonomous. No IT tasks should exist, only business user’s tasks.

    New product characteristics

    Solution was created using a new tool: Power BI (Azure and On-Premise).
    For self-service creation and publication, new business processes where designed and the power user role was created.
    Several repositories where created inside PowerBI for self-service publication. Different corporate segmentations/analysis vectors where taken into account, such as:
    • departments
    • areas
    • product types
    • sales regions, etc.
    The new fast and agile solution empowered Business users and they’re now able to create and share new report and analysis using a corporate tool.
    As wanted, for more than 90% of uses cases no IT task is needed, not even for security management.

    Data Audit

    My role

    20% as Corporate BI Architect
    80% as BI Technical responsible, Scrum Master and Development Team

    Previous existing product characteristics

    No data audit existed.
    Several, mostly manual checks, where done periodically on operational products.
    The cost of these processes was very high and done often.
    There was no integration data auditing (operational products as silos).

    New product requirements (stakeholders view)

    Product owner doesn’t want data to be changed / corrected in analytical solutions but in source operational systems.
    For every data problem or improvement a business process should exist in order to allow changes on data sources.

    New product characteristics

    Created and documented new business processes for data correction or improvement.
    A new audit product was created and is executed after the Analytical execution ends.
    Looking in all Analytical products repositories (Integration, Data Mart, MDM, .etc) the system is able to detect data to be improved/corrected and notifications are send following the business process defined.
    The system is easily managed, scalable and has no impact on the Analytical products execution (as desired).

    Centralized KPI Metadata and Repository

    My role

    20% as Corporate BI Architect
    80% as BI Technical responsible, Scrum Master and Development Team

    Previous existing product characteristics

    There’s no KPI corporate solution, meaning there’s no centralized product for KPI definition, execution, warehousing or documentation.
    There was no single source of truth for KPI’s, so different employees generated similar KPI and Dashboards using personal definition and formulas that don’t match between them.

    New product requirements (stakeholders view)

    In order to have a single source of the truth the company wants a centralized solution for KPI definition and formulas.
    The KPI results will be used in corporate reporting where historical data must exist (for tendencies and historical integrity).
    There’s a need to present the KPI results to some business user through self-service in order for them to use in their own reports.
    Due to the volume and complexity of some KPI the product owner wants then to be pre-calculated (if possible).

    New product characteristics

    Created and documented new business processes for KPI definition and usage.
    Created a centralized KPI Metadata product for:
    • Business definition
    --- Concepts definition
    --- KPI definition
    • Technical definition
    --- Business concepts – Data Mart attributes mapping
    --- KPI calculation formula
    • Execution scheduler
    The KPI Metadata product has one business owner role (1) and several business users (N).
    Created a KPI execution system for daily pre-calculated KPI creation.
    Created a corporate KPI repository inside de Data Mart for saving KPI pre-calculated results. Historical data is maintained.
    Security implemented in all 3 modules using business roles defined on business processes.
